Men

The race of man refers to the race of humankind. According to legend, they were created second (after the elves) by Iluvatar. Men have the "gift of man" which is mortality. This means that they age and die when it is their time.

The most well-known type of man were the Edain, which refers technically to all men but especially those who fought with the elves in the First Age against Morgoth. Both the men of Gondor and Rohan can trace their lineage back to the Edain.
