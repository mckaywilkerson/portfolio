Elves

The race of elves (commonly refered to as the Eldar) were the first and eldest creations of Iluvatar. They are also considered the fairest and wisest of the common races. Elves do not physically age like men, nor do they get sick. However, they can be slain by violence.

In Middle-Earth, during the third-age (which is the age of Lord of the Rings) the elves are starting to diminish in Middle-Earth. After the one ring is destroyed, they fade even further. Most of the elves return to Valinor, while the ones that stay forget and are forgotten.

McKay Wilkerson
