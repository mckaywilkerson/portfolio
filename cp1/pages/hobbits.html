Hobbits

Hobbits (also known as halflings) are known as "relatives" of the race of man; however, they consider themselves to be a separate people. At the end of the third-age, the hobbits mostly resided in the Shire and Bree.

After the destruction of the one ring, they start to move out as far as Gondor and Rohan. The hobbits numbers dwindled after the fourth-age, however it is believed that they have survived even to this day.
