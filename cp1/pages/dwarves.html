Dwarves

The dwarves were created in secret in the first age by Aule, so that he could teach them his crafts. Iluvatar found out and almost destroyed them, but then changed his mind and just decided to hold off on their "awakening" until after the elves were "awakened".

In the middle of the fourth-age (after the one ring was destroyed), the dwarves finally took back Khazid-dum, where they stayed "until the world grew old and the dwarves failed and the days of Durin's race were ended."

McKay Wilkerson
